What is SmartSweeper?
SmartSweeper is a mass marketing tool that allows for the creation and management of a large amount of paper wallets/addresses.
How do I use SmartSweeper?
Create a project > Fund the project > Send the funds from the seed address to the individual addresses > Sweep the funds back when "expired".
Are my funds safe?
You have control of the private key of both your seed/fund address as well as each individually created promotional address. The keys are not sent or transferred anywhere. They are stored locally until you delete the project.
What are the transaction fees?
The fee charged for funding your project depends on the wallet/exchange you use. The fee for funding the promotional wallets with SmartSweeper varies by the number of wallets in the project.
  • 1-10 wallets: Σ0.001
  • 11-50 wallets: Σ0.01
  • 51-100 wallets: Σ0.02
  • 101-200 wallets: Σ0.03
  • 201-300 wallets: Σ0.04
  • 301-400 wallets: Σ0.05
  • 401-500 wallets: Σ0.06
